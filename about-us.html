About Academic Nightingale

Empowering Nigeria's Nursing Professionals Through Excellence in CBT Preparation.

Our Journey & Commitment

Academic Nightingale was founded with a singular vision: to bridge the gap in high-quality, NMCN-standard CBT preparation for nursing and midwifery students across Nigeria. We recognized the challenges aspiring professionals faced in accessing reliable, exam-focused resources and set out to create a platform that not only meets but exceeds these needs.

Our mission is to empower every student with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to excel in their council exams and embark on successful nursing careers. We are driven by a passion for education and a deep respect for the nursing profession.

Since our launch, we've proudly delivered thousands of practice questions, helped countless students accelerate their exam success rates, and become a trusted partner in their journey to licensure.
